About Spider-Man

Peter Parker, an ordinary high school student, was bitten by a radioactive spider during a science exhibition. This fateful event transformed him into Spider-Man, granting him incredible superhuman abilities.

Powers & Abilities: Superhuman strength, agility, and reflexes. Wall-crawling ability. Spider-sense that alerts him to danger. Genius-level intellect in science and engineering.

Suit Technology: Web-shooters designed by Peter himself, capable of creating various types of webbing. Advanced suits incorporate AI assistance, enhanced durability, and specialized functions.

Guided by the principle "With great power comes great responsibility," Spider-Man dedicates his life to protecting New York City from threats both street-level and cosmic.

Movies & Appearances

2002

Spider-Man

Directed by Sam Raimi, starring Tobey Maguire. The origin story that started it all, introducing audiences to Peter Parker's transformation and his battle with the Green Goblin.

2012

The Amazing Spider-Man

Andrew Garfield dons the mask in Marc Webb's reimagining. A new take on the origin story with a focus on Peter's parents and the Lizard threat.

2017

Spider-Man: Homecoming

Tom Holland debuts as Spider-Man in the MCU. A young Peter Parker balances high school life with being an Avenger, facing the Vulture.

2018

Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse

Oscar-winning animated masterpiece introducing Miles Morales. Multiple Spider-People from different dimensions unite to save the multiverse.

2021

The multiverse breaks open! Three generations of Spider-Men unite in this epic conclusion to the Home trilogy, facing classic villains.
